Murgh Kari Recipe

  • 2.5 lbs of chicken thighs
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 whole chopped medium-large onion
  • 1/2 tbsp ginger
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tbsp water
  • 4 tbsp yogurt
  • 20 oz crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp chopped cilantro
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • 1 tsp tumeric
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 tsp garam masala

Throw the onion, garlic and ginger in the pan at a med-high heat and saut√© in 1 tbsp olive oil until translucent (around 8-10 mins)

Add your spices (minus the garam masala). Dry cook the spices for a minute or two to enhance the flavor.

Throw your water in and mix/cook for a minute. Add your tomato and stir better than I did here.Remember to taste here!

While the onion is cooking, chop your chicken into similarly sized pieces. Season with salt, pepper, and curry powder.

Saute chicken on pretty high heat until the color is similar to this. Try not to cook completely, as the chicken will be thrown in at the end to simmer. You'll probably have to do a couple batch to cook all the chicken.

Add your chicken and garam masala, bring to a boil and stir/simmer for a few minutes. Taste again here! If you want more spicyness throw in a little more cayenne.

Stir in your yogurt to your liking. Add chopped coriander and baby you got a stew going! Just kidding you're done cooking. Serve with rice and naan bread good god i fucking love indian food
